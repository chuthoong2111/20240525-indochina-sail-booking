Experiences

From incredible snorkelling and diving to fascinating local tours, there is an activity for everyone at InterContinental Fiji Golf Resort & Spa. You can indulge in a treatment at our award-winning Spa InterContinental, or enjoy a round at our 18-hole championship golf course. Whatever your interests, our team is on hand to ensure you enjoy memorable experiences during your stay.

Spa

Let our therapists and traditional Fijian healers pamper you in our serene Spa InterContinental. Choose from a range of spa experiences drawing on Eastern and Western treatment methods to bring balance to mind, body and soul. Our spa treatments utilise healing native herbs and plants nurtured in our on-site herb garden to provide a quintessentially Fijian way of relaxing.
